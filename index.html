About me

I'm a multi-disciplinary software engineer working in many fields ranging from industry 4.0 applications to solutions for transitioning to electric vehicle (EV) transportation and using web/mobile related technologies for creating new experiences. I like gathering experience in multiple different fields, applying it to my current projects.

As a consultant I'm applying my knowledge to the production and connectivity of tomorrow's world. With my current work I aim to create environmentally friendly solutions to shape the future of transportation.

I am always eager to learn new technologies and never stop challenging the status quo - there is always something that can be improved or innovated upon, and I will try my best to contribute my part in doing so.
